Eligibility Rules

Know if you are eligible to save lives

Blood Donation Eligibility

Criteria Requirement
Age 18 - 65 years
Weight Minimum 45 - 50 kg
Health Must be healthy & no infection
Hemoglobin Normal level required
Donation Gap Men: 3 months
Women: 4 months
Before Donation Eat food, drink water & sleep well

Who Should NOT Donate Blood

Category Conditions
Serious Diseases HIV / AIDS
Hepatitis B or C
Cancer
Heart Disease
Severe Lung Disease
Blood Problems Severe Anemia
Low Hemoglobin
Bleeding Disorders
Temporary Conditions Fever or Infection
Recent Surgery
Pregnancy
Recent Tattoo (6 months)
Uncontrolled BP or Diabetes
